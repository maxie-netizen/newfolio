About Me

I'm a passionate full-stack developer and graphic designer with over 5 years of experience creating beautiful, functional digital solutions. My journey in tech began when I built my first website at 16, and I've been hooked ever since.

My expertise spans both front-end and back-end development, along with professional design skills that allow me to create cohesive, visually appealing applications from concept to completion. I specialize in JavaScript frameworks (React, Vue), Node.js, and Python, with a strong focus on responsive design and user experience.

What sets me apart is my unique combination of technical skills and design sensibility. I don't just write code - I create experiences that users love. Whether it's a complex web application or a simple mobile interface, I approach every project with the same level of care and attention to detail.

When I'm not coding or designing, you can find me contributing to open-source projects, learning new technologies, or sharing knowledge with other developers through mentorship and tech talks.
